KiteSurf Spot

On the southeast coast of Zanzibar, Paje is a breathtaking beach with fluffy white sand, blue waves, and ideal kite-surfing conditions. The beach provides a perfect location for both novice and experienced kitesurfers with regular trade winds that blow almost exactly parallel along the coast. Safe practice is made possible by the large, shallow lagoon; the wind direction guarantees regular rides over clean, warm waves. For kitesurfers all around, Paje is a sought-after location because of its mix of pure beauty and outstanding wind conditions.

Teachers

Kite Summer Center in Paje, Zanzibar, has a skilled and enthusiastic kitesurfing team which enjoys training. They offer customised education for beginners to intermediate riders that put first safety, fun, and speedy growth in a professional yet pleasant approach. Their expertise in local winds and waves and helpful teaching style make kitesurfing on Paje's world-class beach unforgettable.
